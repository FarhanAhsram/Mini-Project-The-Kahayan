About Us

Established since 2000, The Kahayan is the only restaurant in Central Kalimantan that offers indigenous Dayak cuisine in a fine dining ambiance. Located in the heart of Central Kalimantan's capital, Palangka Raya, The Kahayan is a choice for both local and international tourists to indulge in the authentic flavors of the region.

To create an element of surprise, The Kahayan serves different Dayak specialty dishes every day. To maintain the quality of the food's taste, The Kahayan collaborates with Michelin-starred chefs to offer international dishes and a chef from the native Dayak tribe who knows the authentic recipes of various regional dishes. All our ingredients are sourced from local producers to ensure quality and freshness, that's why our menu is seasonal.

The Kahayan has a seating capacity of 140 for standard arrangements and can accommodate up to 200 seats for private events. With a wide range of menu options and comprehensive facilities, we are ready to cater to all your needs. Whether it's a wedding, a birthday, or just a family gathering, we and our entire team at The Kahayan will always provide professional service and make your event more memorable. Enjoy the fine dining experience with local and international menus at The Kahayan.
